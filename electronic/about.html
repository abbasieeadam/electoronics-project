WELCOME TO D-CROWD

Home Of Electronics & Accessories

D-crowd is a Pan-African technology company that is built around a marketplace, logistics service and payment service. The logistics service enables the delivery of packages through a network of local partners while the payment services facilitate the payments of online transactions within D-crowd’s ecosystem.

Parent organization: Rocket Internet
Subsidiary: AFRIBABA Holdings Pte. Ltd.
